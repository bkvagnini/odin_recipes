Homemade BBQ Sauce

Description

It's BBQ Sauce.

In case you don't like the thousand other commercially available BBQ sauces.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 T mustard
  • 2 T worcestershire sauce
  • 1t onion powder
  • 1/2 t black pepper/li>
  • 1/2 t cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 t salt
