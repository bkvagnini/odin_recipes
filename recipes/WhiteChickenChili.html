White Chicken Chili

Description

It's Chili with a twist!

Still a lot of beans, so you'll still be ripping ass while you're watch TV later that evening, but it has chicken instead of beef.

Ingredients

  • 4 regular cans white beans (Navy, Kidney, Pinto, & Garbanzo)
  • 2 fresh jalapeno peppers
  • 2 poblano peppers
  • 1 Large Yellow or White onion
  • 4 teaspoons minced garlic
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (They make a white chicken one, but I prefer the regular chili powder)

Steps

  1. Smash half of the Garbanzo beans & set aside. I'll sometimes add an extra can of Black beans for color.
  2. Mince the jalapeno peppers up.
  3. Chop up the poblano peppers.
  4. Chop up the onion finely.
  5. Cook up 3 skinless breast of chicken with peppers, & seasonings.
  6. Once cooked, shred & set aside.
  7. Add 2 limes, squeeze or juiced into pot.
  8. Add 4 cups Chicken broth
  9. 2 packages of Cream cheeseto the chicken 1/2 hour before serving

I've made this in a Crock Pot as well. Turns out Fantastic!! (From Ken Deemer)
